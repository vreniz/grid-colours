The Band

Noise born from silence and fury.

Void Circuit formed in 2018 in a basement in Detroit. What started as a noise experiment became one of the most visceral live acts of the decade — crossing post-hardcore, industrial metal, and ambient electronics.

  • • 3 studio albums, 1 live record
  • • 200+ shows across 18 countries
  • • Headline slots at Download, Knotfest, Hellfest
  • • New album "Static Black" — July 2026
